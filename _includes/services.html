How I approach UX research and design:

INVESTIGATION

UX is the study of people. I use interviews, field research, and other methods to develop informed questions and understand design problems.

SYNTHESIS

A problem defined is half-solved. I use storytelling and visuals to help build a shared understanding of what is essential and prioritize design efforts around that.

CREATION

I design experiences for humans. From whiteboard boxes-and-arrows sketches to fully interactive prototypes, I always focus on the human narrative in appropriate fidelity.

TESTING

Good ideas benefit from scrutiny. I challenge assumptions so that we build with confidence using A/B tests, analytics, usability testing, expert and heuristic reviews, focus groups and more.
